CALL OF DUTY GHOST
The series of Call of Duty began on the PC and later spread to the x-box.

Call of Duty: Ghosts was released in November 2013. This game was released on Microsoft. It was the first person shooter  game developed by Infinity Ward.

Call of Duty:Ghosts introduces a new game type called Squads. This mode can be played either solo  or with other players including friends. A squad consists of ten different customizable characters.
